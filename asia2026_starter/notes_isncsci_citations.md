ISNCSCI / E-ISNCSCI feature notes (Track 1)

Sources to cite in write-ups or reports:
- International Standards for Neurological Classification of Spinal Cord Injury (ISNCSCI) worksheet and standards (sensory level definition and UEMS/LEMS scoring).
- E-ISNCSCI expedited exam guidance (minimal steps to determine NLI and AIS; motivates stop-depth and sacral sparing proxies).
- SCIRE or other clinical summaries that restate ISNCSCI motor scoring conventions (UEMS/LEMS totals).
